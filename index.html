Short Bio

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Qi Dou since Fall 2023. Previously, I obtained my B.Eng. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Shenyuan Honors College, Beihang University (BUAA) in 2023. During my undergraduate studies, I worked as a research intern at the Chinese Institute for Brain Research, Beijing (CIBR), under the supervision of Dr. Zaixu Cui.

My research interests include dexterous manipulation, 3D vision for robotics, and embodied AI.
